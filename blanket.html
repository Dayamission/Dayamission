Blanket Distribution

Showing God's love through practical care

Bringing Warmth and Hope

Daya Mission helps people living in regions where the climate is extremely cold. Many families in these areas are poor and vulnerable. During severe winter, elderly people and children are often the first affected.

Providing warm clothes and blankets is a simple but meaningful way of showing God's love and care.

The need is great, and we invite you to join hands with Daya Mission to serve those in need.
