Blanket Distribution

Daya Mission helps people living in regions where winters are extremely cold. Poverty and disease often increase during this season, especially among the elderly.

We provide warm blankets and clothing as a simple but meaningful way of showing care. Many villagers receive these gifts with tears of gratitude.

The need is great and urgent. We invite you to support this work and help bring warmth and hope.

Education & Gifts for Children

Children respond with joy when they receive small gifts. We distribute school bags, pens, pencils, notebooks, and stationery during village outreach.

We also conduct educational classes in remote villages where no formal education exists. These resources help children continue their studies and build a better future.
