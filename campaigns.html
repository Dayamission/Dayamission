Blanket Distribution

Daya Mission serves families living in regions where winters are severe and many struggle with poverty. During the cold season, thousands are left vulnerable without adequate clothing or blankets.

Through our Blanket Distribution Campaign, we provide warm blankets and clothing to elderly people, widows, children, and families in need. What may seem like a simple gift often becomes a life-saving blessing.

Every blanket we distribute is an expression of Christ's love and compassion. Many recipients are deeply touched by this practical demonstration of God's care.
