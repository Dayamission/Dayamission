Welcome To Daya Mission

Daya Mission is a Christian ministry committed to sharing the love of Jesus Christ, bringing hope to communities and serving people who are suffering and struggling.

In 2015, God placed a burden in our hearts for lost souls and we began reaching out to the Eastern states of India. A small group of missionaries was selected and Daya Mission was born.

Through challenges, trials and difficulties, the Lord continued to open doors. The faithful preaching of God's Word produced a harvest of souls because the Lord worked with them.
