Education & Gifts For Children

Building hope through education

Helping Children Dream

Daya Mission believes every child deserves encouragement, education and hope.

As part of our ministry, we distribute school bags, notebooks, pens, pencils and educational materials to children in remote villages.

In places where educational facilities are limited, these simple gifts bring joy and motivation to continue learning.
