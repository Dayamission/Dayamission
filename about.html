Welcome To Daya Mission

Daya Mission is a Christian ministry committed to sharing the love of Jesus Christ, bringing hope to communities and serving people who are suffering and struggling.

In 2015, God placed a burden in our hearts for lost souls and Daya Mission was born to reach the Eastern states of India.

Through challenges, trials and difficulties, the Lord continued to open doors, and the faithful preaching of His Word produced a harvest of souls.
