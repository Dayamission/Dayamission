About Us

Welcome to Daya Mission

At Daya Mission, we believe that if there is love, unity and cooperation among people, then we can lead those who suffer and struggle towards peace.

In the early days, God gave us a burden for lost souls and we decided to reach the eastern states of India. In 2015, a small group of missionaries was selected, and Daya Mission was born.

The missionaries faced many obstacles, trials, and persecution while starting the work. Yet faithful preaching continued, and the Lord worked with them, confirming His word through signs that followed.
