Welcome to Daya Mission

At Daya Mission, we believe that love, unity, and cooperation can bring peace to those who suffer and struggle.

In 2015, God gave us a burden for lost souls and a small group of missionaries began the work in the eastern states of India.

Despite trials and persecution, God worked powerfully and confirmed His word with signs following.
