About Daya Mission

Sharing Christ's Love • Bringing Hope • Transforming Lives

Our Story

Daya Mission is a Christian ministry committed to sharing the love of Jesus Christ, serving communities and bringing hope to people who are suffering and struggling.

In the early days, God placed a burden in our hearts for lost souls, and we were led to reach out to the Eastern states of India.

In 2015, a small group of missionaries were selected and Daya Mission was born.

The beginning was not easy. The missionaries faced many obstacles, challenges, trials and persecutions as the ministry was established in small communities.

Yet, the faithful preaching of God's Word began to produce a harvest of souls because the Lord worked with them and confirmed His Word through transformed lives.

Our Vision

To see lives transformed through the love of Jesus Christ and bring hope to communities through faith, compassion and service.

Our Mission

To proclaim the Gospel, serve people in need, strengthen believers and demonstrate God's love through practical acts of kindness.
